1. Download G-Launcher from: https://gdevs.io .

2. Sign in to the launcher with your Mojang account.

3. Select the + button in the Botton left corner of the G-Launcher window.

4. Select the import pack tab in the popup window.

5. Paste the following link into the text box:
https://gameplex-software.github.io/CDN/project%20releases/mods/private/Cruel%20World%20Survival-10.1.2.zip

8. The pack will now start to import (this may take several minutes to complete)

9. After the pack finishes importing you may be asked to give it a name, call it whatever you like, and G-Launcher will download all the mods required

10. once the download has finished you will see your pack on the screen, simply click it to launch.
